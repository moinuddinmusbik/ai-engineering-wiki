# Meta

Tech company and major open-source AI model provider. Creator of the Llama model family and PyTorch.

## Key Contributions (from wiki sources)

- **Llama model family** — Open-weight models that prioritized usability over raw performance:
  - Llama 1: 1.4T training tokens
  - Llama 2: 2T tokens, RLHF for alignment
  - Llama 3: 15T tokens, switched to DPO, context length expanded to 128K
  - Llama 3-405B: 126 transformer blocks, largest open model
- **Practical tradeoff**: Chose smaller models than Chinchilla-optimal for cheaper inference and wider adoption.
- **PyTorch** — Deep learning framework used across the industry.

## Relevance

Represents the open-source/open-weight approach to [[Foundation Models]], in contrast to the closed API approach of [[OpenAI]] and [[Anthropic]].
