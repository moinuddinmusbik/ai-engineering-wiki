# OpenAI

AI research company and model provider. Creator of the GPT series, ChatGPT, CLIP, and Sora.

## Key Facts

- Released GPT-1 (117M params, June 2018), GPT-2 (1.5B params, Feb 2019), GPT-4 (2023).
- ChatGPT's success was the catalyst for the AI engineering boom.
- Pioneered the model-as-a-service approach — exposing models via APIs.
- GPT-4 vocabulary size: 100,256 tokens.
- CEO: Sam Altman, who stated the biggest opportunity for most people will be adapting foundation models for specific applications.

## Relevance

Central to the [[AI Engineering]] narrative. OpenAI's API-first approach lowered the barrier to building AI apps, directly enabling the discipline. Their models (GPT series) are the most-referenced throughout the book.
