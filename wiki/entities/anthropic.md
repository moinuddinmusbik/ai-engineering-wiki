# Anthropic

AI safety company and model provider. Creator of the Claude model family.

## Key Facts

- Listed among the "ambitious, well-funded startups" alongside [[OpenAI]] and Mistral that can afford to develop [[Foundation Models]].
- Claude 2 scored 78.5% on MMLU (5-shot), compared in the Gemini technical report.
- Claude 3 mentioned as a multimodal model capable of understanding images and text.

## Relevance

One of the key model providers in the [[AI Engineering]] ecosystem. Represents the competitive landscape of foundation model development.
