# Chip Huyen

Author of *AI Engineering* (O'Reilly, 2024). Writer and engineer focused on AI/ML systems in production.

## Key Facts

- Wrote the book that defines AI engineering as a discipline distinct from traditional ML engineering.
- Interviewed 50+ companies on their AI strategies and analyzed 205 open-source AI applications for the book.
- Maintains a regularly updated list of open-source AI applications on GitHub.
- Advisor of Convai (3D conversational AI). Investor in Photoroom.
- Previously authored *Designing Machine Learning Systems* (O'Reilly).

## Relevance

Primary author and voice of the [[AI Engineering]] framework this wiki is built around. Her perspective blends practical production experience with broad industry research.
